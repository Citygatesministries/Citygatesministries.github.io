About the Ranch

 Mission and Vision

Vision: To forge a spiritual framework that develops leadership, provide opportunities to serve God, affirm an identity in the light of Christ.

Mission: Make clear the moral implication of God’s will; break down illusions and inspire change; share the faith as Christian Conciseness; and connect human consequences with spiritual life skills that result in transformation. 

 Who We Are

We are a Christ centered sober living facility with a heart to serve God and our local community. Our pastor is committed to minster a message of spiritual renewal to men who have lost sight of their identity in Christ. Simply put we ranch disciples as sheep that the good Lord may raise them as spiritual leaders at home.

 What we Do

Support local church communities to help minster to men in need of Spiritual Renewal. Awakening has been established to:    
        *Meet the needs of extended discipleship
        *Sharpen bible skills for continued study
        *Instill principles of Biblical Ethics
         *Help develop leadership qualities as life skills.   

Building Leaders through Sound Doctrine 

Awakening Ranch is place where men are discipled to learn and apply Faithful Stewardship and Biblical Ethics to their Christian walk.

12 Months of Spiritual Renewal for Advanced Discipleship

A one year commitment has three phases which include integrated work ethic to help disciples develop a feeling of usefulness. Upon completion our Awakening men are prepared to lead spiritually at home and the work place.

Easy and Simple to Use

Teaching clusters are available for those interested. Contact Pastor George to receive information about serving God from your Church at the Awakening Ranch.

Statement of Faith

“Compete well for the faith and lay hold of that eternal life you were called for and made your good confession for in the presence of many witnesses. ” (1 Timothy 6:12, 13)

We believe in:

The Authority of scripture: God’s word is inerrant; His scripture is Holy, inspired (God Breathed) of him, infallible, conveying the highest moral standard.
The Person of God: There is one and only one living and true God. …The eternal triune God reveals Himself to us as Father, Son and Holy Spirit, with distinct personal attributes, but without division of nature, essence, or being.
The Creation of Mankind: In God’s image, male and female, a sinner by Adam and Eve; worthy of Christian love and respect.
The Truth: Eternal redemption is given by the atoning value of God’s blood to regenerate, justify, sanctify, and glorify.
God ‘s Purpose: That by the grace of God and His election of spiritually adopted believers accepted in Christ and sanctified by Holy Spirit may never to fall away from the state of grace may endure to the end.
The Church: Is made up of a group that are called out from among the world in a convent of faith and fellowship of the gospel to exercise the gifts, rights and privileges outlined in Holy Scripture to share the faith to whatever extent God commands. Every believer is responsible and accountable to Christ as Lord. Its scriptural officers are gifted to serve the spiritual needs of others and the office of pastor is limited to men as qualified by Scripture.
Baptism: Is an outward showing of an inward work symbolizing the believer’s faith in a crucified, buried, and risen Savior, the believer’s death to sin and the new life in Christ Jesus.
Christ Return: In God’s time Christ will judge mankind as to unrighteousness whom will be consigned to Hell with those righteous ones to receive everlasting life a glorified body in the presents of the Lord.
God’s Blessings: The sacredness of what God gives either temporal or spiritual places us in a state of binding stewardship as we are to serve Him with our time, talents and material possessions.
The Ordained Family: Beginning with the uniting of one man and one woman the marriage is a Holy institution of God. The husband has the God-given responsibility to provide for, protect, and take the spiritual lead for his family. The wife is to submit the husband’s leadership as the church does to Christ, as a helper she is responsible to offer respect, manage the house, and nurture those under it. At the moment of conception children are a blessing and heritage from the Lord. Parents are obligated to show them the way God loves in marriage and Church.
